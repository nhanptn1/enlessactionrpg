LEVEL-UP, XP, AND WAVE PROGRESSION PLAN - Vertical Endless Archer RPG

1) Progression Goal
- Keep the game easy to understand.
- Make each wave feel slightly harder than the last.
- Reward the player often with upgrades.
- Boss waves should feel like milestones.
- Endless mode should continue scaling without stopping.

2) XP System
- Enemies drop XP when defeated.
- Different enemy types can give different XP values.
- Basic enemies give small XP.
- Elites give medium XP.
- Bosses give large XP.
- XP bar fills toward next level.

3) Level-Up Timing
- Early levels should come fast.
- Later levels should take longer.
- Example: level 1 to 2 is easy, level 10 to 11 takes more XP.
- This keeps early game fun and late game controlled.

4) Level-Up Rewards
When the player levels up, show 3 random choices:
- Increase damage.
- Reduce cooldown.
- Increase arrow count.
- Increase projectile speed.
- Increase area size.
- Increase critical hit chance.
- Gain HP.
- Gain shield.
- Improve XP gain.

5) Skill Unlock Plan
- Level 1: basic attack only.
- Level 3: unlock Multishot.
- Level 5: unlock Piercing Arrow.
- Level 8: unlock Arrow Rain or Trap Shot.
- Later levels: upgrade skill strength or reduce cooldown.

6) Wave Structure
- Waves 1 to 3: easy waves.
- Waves 4 to 6: introduce new enemy types.
- Waves 7 to 9: add elites and more mixed groups.
- Wave 10: boss wave.
- After wave 10, continue with stronger stats and more enemies.

7) Endless Scaling Rules
- Enemy HP increases every wave or every few waves.
- Enemy speed increases slowly.
- Enemy count increases gradually.
- Elite spawn chance increases over time.
- Bosses gain new phase behavior in later cycles.

8) Difficulty Curve
Early game:
- Fewer enemies.
- Slow speed.
- Fast level-ups.
- Simple choices.

Mid game:
- More mixed enemies.
- More elites.
- Slightly faster waves.
- More important upgrade choices.

Late game:
- Dense enemy groups.
- Faster enemies.
- Strong elites.
- Bosses with stronger patterns.
- Survival becomes the main challenge.

9) Boss Wave Structure
- Boss appears every 10 waves.
- Normal enemies stop spawning or spawn very lightly during boss fight.
- Boss has 2 to 4 attack patterns.
- Boss may summon adds in phase 2 or phase 3.
- Boss defeat ends the wave and gives large XP.

10) Upgrade Categories
A. Offense
- Damage.
- Crit chance.
- Attack speed.
- Arrow count.
- Projectile speed.

B. Defense
- HP.
- Shield.
- Damage reduction.
- Healing bonus.

C. Utility
- Cooldown reduction.
- XP gain bonus.
- Movement speed.
- Pickup range.

D. Skill Modifiers
- Extra projectile.
- Bigger area.
- Longer duration.
- Chain effect.
- Pierce count.

11) Example Wave Reward Loop
- Clear wave.
- Gain XP.
- Level up if XP is full.
- Choose a new upgrade.
- Prepare for the next wave.
- Continue until boss wave.

12) Recommended Balance Approach
- Do not make enemies too strong too early.
- Let the player feel powerful for the first few waves.
- Increase difficulty only after the player has time to learn the system.
- Give meaningful rewards often enough to keep the run exciting.

13) MVP Progression Set
- 1 XP bar.
- 1 level-up popup.
- 8 possible upgrades.
- 4 skill unlock steps.
- 10-wave boss cycle.
- Endless wave scaling.

14) Future Expansion Ideas
- Permanent meta progression.
- Character classes.
- Relics and gear.
- Random run modifiers.
- Daily challenge mode.
- Achievement rewards.
