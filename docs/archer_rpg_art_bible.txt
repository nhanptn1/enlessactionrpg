ART BIBLE - Vertical Endless Archer RPG

1) Overall Art Style
- Top-down pixel art fantasy.
- Clean silhouettes and readable shapes.
- Slightly dark adventure mood, not horror.
- Consistent sprite scale across characters, enemies, and items.
- Use the same outline thickness and shading style for all assets.

2) Main Theme
- Forest fantasy / ruined kingdom.
- Nature, corruption, and ancient magic.
- Player is a ranger archer fighting waves of monsters.
- Visual tone: adventurous, mystical, and energetic.

3) Color Palette
- Primary: green, brown, gold.
- Secondary: blue, gray, white.
- Danger accents: red, dark purple, black.
- UI highlight colors: gold and cyan.
- Boss colors: dark red, black, purple, or corrupted green.

4) Player Character - Archer
- Role: forest ranger / hunter.
- Silhouette: slim, agile, readable bow shape.
- Clothes: hood, short cloak, leather armor, boots, gloves.
- Colors: green cloak, brown leather, gold trim.
- Signature detail: always visible bow and quiver.
- Animation focus: idle, shoot, dash, hit, special skill.

5) Monster Design
- Small enemies: slimes, bats, goblins, insects.
- Medium enemies: skeleton warriors, wolves, masked bandits.
- Tank enemies: armored brute, stone beast, shield skeleton.
- Elite enemies: glowing or corrupted versions of normal enemies.
- Bosses: corrupted guardian, demon beast, dark ranger, giant knight.
- Rule: make every enemy readable by silhouette first.

6) Item Design
- Weapons: bows, daggers, short swords, staffs.
- Armor: leather vest, cloak, chestplate, boots.
- Accessories: ring, amulet, charm, bracelet.
- Consumables: potion, elixir, bomb, scroll.
- Style: simple fantasy icons with strong contrast.
- Inventory icons should be easy to read at small size.

7) UI Style
- Dark fantasy panels.
- Parchment or stone frame look.
- Gold or blue borders for selected elements.
- Rounded square inventory slots.
- Skill buttons with cooldown overlays.
- Boss HP bar with strong warning color.

8) Boss Visual Style
- Larger silhouette than normal enemies.
- Strong color contrast.
- More details around head, weapon, or glowing core.
- Use particles, aura, or shadow effects.
- Boss design should look dangerous even in a small sprite.

9) Animation Style
- Keep animations short and clear.
- 4 to 6 frames is enough for many actions.
- Movement should feel sharp and readable.
- Attack animation should show impact quickly.
- Death animation should be simple and fast.

10) Asset Search Targets
- Top-down pixel RPG starter pack.
- Fantasy archer sprite pack.
- Top-down monster pack.
- RPG UI pack.
- Item icon pack.
- Look for assets that match the same pixel size and lighting.

11) Best Design Choice
- Hero: forest ranger archer.
- Enemies: corrupted woodland creatures and undead.
- Items: classic RPG gear icons.
- UI: parchment with gold trim.
- Mood: adventure, nature, corruption, and magic.

12) Practical Rule
- One visual language for all assets.
- Same pixel resolution.
- Same color brightness level.
- Same level of detail.
- Keep the game simple, readable, and polished.
