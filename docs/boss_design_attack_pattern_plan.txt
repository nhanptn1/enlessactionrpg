BOSS DESIGN AND ATTACK PATTERN PLAN - Vertical Endless Archer RPG

1) Boss Design Goal
- Bosses should feel like the main challenge of every 10 waves.
- Boss fights should be readable, fair, and exciting.
- Each boss should have unique attacks and a strong visual identity.
- Bosses should create a clear change in game pace.

2) Boss Design Rules
- Bigger sprite or model than normal enemies.
- Strong silhouette and color contrast.
- 2 to 4 attack patterns for MVP.
- Clear telegraph before each attack.
- Different phase behavior when HP is reduced.
- Reward the player with large XP or upgrade after defeat.

3) Boss Phase Structure
Phase 1:
- Basic attacks.
- Slow movement or mostly stationary.
- Easy to learn patterns.

Phase 2:
- Faster attacks.
- Extra projectiles or summons.
- More pressure on the player.

Phase 3:
- Low HP enraged state.
- Faster movement or shorter cooldowns.
- Final attack sequence before defeat.

4) Boss Concept A: Corrupted Forest Guardian
Visual:
- Huge plant-stone creature.
- Dark green, brown, black, glowing purple.
- Roots, vines, and cracked stone body.

Attacks:
- Root Slam: hits the ground in front.
- Vine Whip: long-range sweeping attack.
- Poison Burst: area attack around boss.
- Summon Saplings: spawns small minions.

Phases:
- Phase 1: root slam and vine whip.
- Phase 2: poison burst and summon saplings.
- Phase 3: faster vine attacks and enraged movement.

5) Boss Concept B: Dark Ranger Commander
Visual:
- Large enemy archer with cloak and horned mask.
- Black, red, silver, or dark purple color scheme.
- Bow glows with cursed energy.

Attacks:
- Rapid Volley: several arrows in a fan spread.
- Aimed Shot: strong single attack with warning line.
- Arrow Rain: attacks multiple points from above.
- Shadow Step: short teleport or dash.

Phases:
- Phase 1: aimed shot and rapid volley.
- Phase 2: arrow rain and shadow step.
- Phase 3: faster volley and mixed attack chains.

6) Boss Concept C: Fallen Knight
Visual:
- Heavy armored knight.
- Broken shield, giant sword, cursed aura.
- Steel, dark blue, red glow.

Attacks:
- Sword Slash: close frontal attack.
- Charge: rushes forward quickly.
- Shockwave: heavy ground attack.
- Shield Burst: defensive counter attack.

Phases:
- Phase 1: sword slash and charge.
- Phase 2: shockwave and shield burst.
- Phase 3: faster charge chains and stronger shockwave.

7) Boss Concept D: Demon Beast
Visual:
- Large horned beast.
- Red, black, orange, fire effects.
- Claws, teeth, smoke, and glowing eyes.

Attacks:
- Claw Swipe: fast close-range hit.
- Fire Breath: cone attack.
- Leap Smash: jumps to a target area.
- Summon Flames: creates dangerous zones.

Phases:
- Phase 1: claw swipe and fire breath.
- Phase 2: leap smash and summon flames.
- Phase 3: rage state with faster combos.

8) Telegraph Design
- Show warning markers before heavy attacks.
- Use glowing lines, circles, or floor cracks.
- Boss should never hit without warning in MVP.
- Good telegraphing makes difficult fights feel fair.

9) Boss Arena Behavior
- Boss wave should clear most normal enemies.
- The screen should focus on the boss.
- Add limited support enemies only if needed.
- Boss arena should feel more intense than normal waves.

10) Attack Pattern Examples
- Single shot.
- Fan spread.
- Area slam.
- Summon minions.
- Dash attack.
- Line attack.
- Circle explosion.
- Multi-phase combo.

11) Boss Difficulty Scaling
- Boss HP increases every 10-wave cycle.
- Attack speed increases in later cycles.
- Boss gains one extra pattern after certain milestones.
- Boss movement speed can also increase slightly.

12) Boss Rewards
- Large XP gain.
- Level-up bonus or guaranteed upgrade choice.
- Gold or currency reward.
- Chance for rare item or relic.
- Optional healing after victory.

13) MVP Boss Set
For the first version, build only one boss:
- Corrupted Forest Guardian.
- 3 attack patterns.
- 2 phases.
- 1 summon move.
- Clear telegraph lines.
- Big reward after defeat.

14) Future Boss Expansion
- Add more bosses for each difficulty tier.
- Add elemental bosses.
- Add elite mini-boss enemies.
- Add boss mutations for endless mode.
- Add special boss events every 30 or 50 waves.
